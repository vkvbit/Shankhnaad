Bibliophile

(The Sameekshak)











Introduction


The participants can choose any novel of their choice and prepare a review of that. They have to present their review in front of the audience as well as the judging panel.



The review should contain


  1. An introduction to the author(s), including the author's title and place of work, and some indication of who the author is.
  2. A brief summary of the novel along with an introduction to the main theme of the novel and description of its content.
  3. An evaluation of the novel's merits, usefulness, and special contributions, along with shortcomings you think are necessary to point out.
  4. The review should be short but descriptive. Each participant will be given 10 minutes for his review.
  5. The review should introduce the audience to the book's content and focus on the subject of the book being reviewed.
