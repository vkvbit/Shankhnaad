Tech Shayar











Introduction


The participants will have to write as well as recite a poem based on engineering lifestyle. They are also allowed to share their personal views, opinions and experience related to engineering.



Rules


  1. Each poem must be an original piece performed by the participant/group.
  2. Performances will be timed and should not exceed three (3) minutes of time duration. There will be a random draw for performance order.
  3. There will be no restriction for language. Participants can use whatever language they are comfortable with but it should be understandable by the audience.
  4. Heckling or disrupting of another performer's performance results in disqualification.
  5. Use of abusive words during performance will result in immediate disqualification.
