BioTeaser Hunt

(Quiz Competition)











Introduction


Game or mind sport in which players attempt to answer questions correctly .To evaluate the knowledge of the participants within academics as well as beyond academics.



Event Structure


  1. Event includes two quiz rounds which will test your knowledge on biotechnology.
  2. Question paper will include 45 objective questions There will be time limit of 30 minutes.



Scoring Method


Every correct answer will be awarded with 1 mark. There will be a no negative marking.



Guidelines


No one is allowed to bring any electronic gadget, book or a piece of paper in event room.



Judging Criteria


Mark will be awarded on the correctness of the answer. In case of tie , bonus question will be the deciding criteria. Decision of judges will be final.
