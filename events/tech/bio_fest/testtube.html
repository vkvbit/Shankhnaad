TestTube Buddies

(Micro-Flora Art)











Introduction


Germ art or agar art or microbial art is the same thing in which artwork is created by culturing microorganisms in certain patterns. The microbes used can be bacteria, yeast, fungi, or protists (less commonly).



Event Structure


Agar plates without any contamination will be passed to next round.
The candidates have to performed striking (bacterial art) provided on the spot.



Rules & Regulations


  1. Only two petri plate will be provided during the event.
  2. Single use of Laminar Air Flow will be allowed. No second chance will be given.
  3. Maximum time will be allowed is 45 minutes.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. The clear and artistic word design will be selected. The final decision will be taken by the judges of expert panel.
