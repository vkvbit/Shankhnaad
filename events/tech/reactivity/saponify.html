Saponify











Introduction


In this event , the competitors have to put the theoretical concepts into practical use. The participants were supposed to make soap. Participants were advised to make soap with minimum chemicals and natural products.



Rules & Regulations


  1. The competition is open to all the students.
  2. Each team has maximum three members.
  3. Misbehave with coordinator can directly result into disqualification.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. Production of good quality of soap with minimum chemical within given time.
