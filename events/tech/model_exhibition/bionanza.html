Bionanza

(Model Presentation)











Introduction


Model presentation event that includes working model i.e., physical presentation of a topics that shows how it works related to biotechnology field.



Event Structure


ROUND 1 (SCREENING):

  1. The participating teams have to submit an abstract focusing on their model idea.
  2. The abstract must be in pdf format.
  3. The selection will be based on abstract submitted.
  4. All abstracts must be submitted electronically with subject “Abstract submission”.
  5. Abstract should be send on Mail Id- biofest2022@gmail.com

ROUND 2 (PRESENTATION):

  1. All the shortlisted teams will be asked to present their model in Tech wizard 2022 at DR. A.I.T.H.



Rules & Regulations


  1. Maximum five members in a team are allowed.
  2. The model must be a working model.
  3. Presence of each member of team is mandatory at the time of presentation round.
  4. Abstract should not be longer than 200 words involving problem statement, idea proposal, model’s blueprint, result and conclusion.
  5. All models should be related to biotechnology field.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. Models will be judged by the way of presentation and explanation.
  2. Cost effective and efficient model will be preferred.
  3. Final decision will be taken by judges of expert panel.
