Maze Solving








Introduction


Now a days, maze solving problem is an important field of robotics. It is based on one of the most important areas of robot, which is “Decision Making Algorithm”. Cause, this robot will be placed in unknown place, and it requires to have a good decision-making capability. There are many types of mazes solving robot using various type of algorithms. In this project, the system design of Maze solving robot consist obstacle avoidance ultrasonic sensors and then sensors will detect the wall. To solve the maze, this robot will apply wall following algorithms such as left right hand rule. It will also follow the flood fill algorithm for the shortest path.



Rules & Regulations


  1. Disturbing other participants or coordinator will result in disqualification.
  2. A team can have maximum 4 members. Students from different educational institutes can form a team.
  3. Each team has to design two manually controlled robots either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can travel through all turns of the track.
  4. Robot will start on the start point and will move forward by following the right path based on the intelligence of the bot in the shortest time possible.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. On the basis of time taken to solve the maze.
  2. Decision of judge’s will be final.
