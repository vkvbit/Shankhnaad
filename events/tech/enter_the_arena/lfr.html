LFR

(Line Follower Robot)








Introduction


A line follower robot is basically a robot designed to follow a ‘line’ or path already predetermined by the user. This line or path may be as simple as a physical white line on the floor or as complex path marking schemes e.g., embedded lines, magnetic markers and laser guide markers. In order to detect these specific markers or ‘lines’ various sensing schemes can be applied. The choice of these schemes would be dependent upon the sensing accuracy and flexibility required.



Rules & Regulations


  1. A team can have maximum 4 members.
  2. Students from different educational institutes can form a team.
  3. Each team has to design two manually controlled robots either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can travel through all turns of the track.
  4. Robots will start on the START point and fill all shapes by traveling through various laps and hurdles in the shortest time.
  5. The robot that will complete the specified task in the least time will be the winner.
  6. The competition area has a special place defined for the robot's operation.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. On the basis of time taken.
  2. On the basis of no. of check post cleared.
  3. Decision of judge’s will be final.
