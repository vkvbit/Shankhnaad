Fiery Lyric

(Rap Competition)











Introduction


Rapping (also rhyming) is a musical form of vocal delivery that incorporates “rhyme, rhythmic speech, and street vernacular”,accompaniment which is performed or chanted in a variety of ways, usually over a backing beat or musical accompaniment. In recent times, apart from being a form of entertainment, Rap has become an energetic & vibrant form of music which is used to deliver a message in an impactful & bold manner. In order to celebrate the power of this enthusiastic form of music, we Sargam-The Music Club, are organizing a Rap contest in the name of 'Fiery lyric'.



Rules & Regulations


  1. Category: Rap singing
  2. Organizing club: Sargam- The Music Club.
  3. Team status: S bm gvvolo
  4. No. Of Rounds: 01
  5. Time limit: 1.5- 2 minutes.
  6. The participants can perform a cover rap or can perform their original/self- written composition.
  7. Beat/karaoke of the rap needs to be submitted before hand to the organizing authority.
  8. Participants should ensure that the lyrics should not be have unrefined or indecent words/phrases.
  9. All the participants should be college students at present & should posses a valid college ID card.
  10. Every participant must follow the official Instagram page of Sargam- The Music Club of our college (UserID: sargam.aith) for all the updates.
  11. The decision of the jury would be final & abiding for all.
  12. All the participants should prepare themselves according to the criteria set for the competition & should abide by each & every rule mentioned above, failing which would result in strict disciplinary action by the organizing authority.



Criteria of Judgement


  1. Lyrics delivery(*Bonus points may be awarded for original lyrics & composition)
  2. Beat/timing
  3. Confidence/attitude
  4. Persuasive elements
  5. Diction
