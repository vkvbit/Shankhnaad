Mr. & Miss Shankhnaad

(Fashion & modelling contest)











Introduction


Fashion is a form of self-expression and autonomy at a particular period and place and in a specific context, of clothing, footwear, lifestyle, accessories, makeup, hairstyle, and body posture. The term implies a look defined by the fashion industry as that which is trending. Everything that is considered fashion is available and popularized by the fashion system.
An appropriate sense of fashion along with a well groomed personality give rise to a wonderful form of art well known to us ‘Modelling’. We the team hindgala brings fourth an amazing fashion and modelling contest in the name of Mr./Miss Shankhnaad.



Rules & Regulations


  1. Number of rounds: 2
  2. Round 1: Funky round
  3. Round 2: Ethnic round
  4. For the first round, the participants have to give a unique & creative introduction of themselves along with a ramp walk.
  5. The details of second round shall be revealed after the completion of first round.
  6. Every participant must follow the official Instagram page of Hind Gala- The fashion club of our college (UserID: hind_gala_2k21) for all the updates.
  7. A two round grooming session will be provided to all the participants by an expert.
  8. All the participants should be college students at present & should posses a valid college ID card.
  9. The decision of the jury would be final & abiding for all.
  10. All the participants should prepare themselves according to the criteria set for the competition & should abide by each & every rule mentioned above, failing which would result in strict disciplinary action by the organizing authority.



Specific Rules for Boys


  1. Minimum Height required - 5’7”
  2. Dress code for round one – Blue Denim, White T-shirt.
  3. Dress code for round two – Ethnic wear like Blazer(3-piece/2-piece)/ Sherwaani/Kurta pajama etc.
  4. Every participant should carry a pair of formal as well as casual shoes.
  5. All the participants should bring their own make up kits & Oranamts(if required).



Specific Rules for Girls


  1. Minimum Height required - 5’2”
  2. Dress code for round one - Blue Denim , White T-shirt.
  3. Dress code for round two – Ethnic wear like Gown,Saree etc.
  4. Every participant should carry a pair of heels with minimum heel of 4 inches
  5. Every participant should carry their own makeup kit and ornaments/jewellery
